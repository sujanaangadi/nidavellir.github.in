# POWERHOUSE & RENEWABLE ENERGY SYSTEM ## 10 Key Problems Solved --- ### Dijkstra's Algo **Shortest Path Navigation** Calculating the fastest route for renewable‑energy vehicles from point A to B considering dynamic edge weights (traffic, terrain, charging points). --- ### Ford‑Fulkerson **Max Energy Flow Optimization** Determining the maximum capacity of power routes in the smart‑grid network to ensure stable electricity distribution. --- ### BFS **Grid Connectivity Check** Using Breadth‑First Search to ensure all power stations in the distribution network are reachable from the control hub. --- ### Cycle Detection **Deadlock Prevention in Power Routing** Detecting circular dependencies in the routing of electricity across high‑demand zones to prevent overload loops. --- ### Greedy **Renewable Storage Allocation** Optimizing how solar and wind energy is allocated to storage units in real‑time to minimize waste. --- ### Dynamic Programming **Optimal Energy Scheduling** Balancing consumption vs. production across the grid using DP to minimize blackout probability. --- ### Linear Programming **Cost‑Efficient Power Distribution** Using LP to determine the cheapest way to route energy across transmission lines. --- ### Heuristic Search **Smart EV Charging Optimization** Ensuring electric vehicles receive optimal charging schedules during peak hours. --- ### Clustering **Weather‑Driven Energy Prediction** Grouping similar weather patterns to predict future renewable‑energy generation. --- ### Regression Models **Power Demand Forecasting** Forecasting electricity demand for upcoming hours/days to reduce load stress on the system.
